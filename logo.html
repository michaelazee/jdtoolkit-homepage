Logo

For the logo assignment, I decided to create a logo for my fashion platform I'm currently building called Kitlin Couture. I decided to draw a very simple hanger for my platform's symbol, as well as use colors that are very clean and simple. My favorite color is also purple, which is why I decided to make the text that color. In addition, I added the "outer glow" effect under the Stylize section to give my logo a softer appearance.
