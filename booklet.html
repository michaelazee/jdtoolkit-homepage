Booklet

For the booklet assignment, I composed a compilation of articles I wrote about fashion in New York City. Most of these were written earlier this year, so many of these stories focus on how the coronavirus pandemic affected the fashion industry.

Several of the images were taken during New York Fashion Week, while the others feature me and my boyfriend for a photo assignment I had to do for my fashion journalism course last semester.
